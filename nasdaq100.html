NASDAQ and Nibbāna

Nibbāna is a Pāḷi word for an ultimate reality that most of us will never know but should. Experiencing it is an essential stop on the way to extinguishment, the ultimate goal of Buddhist spiritual practice.

It's way easier to know the components of the NASDAQ 100 and speculate on stock prices than it is to know Nibbāna. That doesn't mean we still shouldn't try to know Nibbāna.

According to the Tipiṭaka, the first step toward that knowledge is developing the habit of giving happily to meritorious people.

Mitzvah Capital was formed to help develop that habit, and turn the stupidity of staring at heatmaps of the NASDAQ100 in hopes of accruing capital into an activity that reminds us that there are more worthwhile pursuits.
